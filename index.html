About Digwydd Dev

Hello! I'm Morgan Huw, an independent developer based in the beautiful country of Wales. At Digwydd Dev, my focus is on crafting practical, reliable software solutions that truly make a difference for your business.

With a solid background in IT support and extensive hands-on development experience, I understand the importance of robust and user-friendly systems. A core service I offer is code reviewing and debugging, ensuring your existing projects are optimized and free of issues.

I've had the privilege of building impactful solutions, including a custom booking system that transformed operations for a travel agency and a comprehensive visitor management system for schools, enhancing both efficiency and security.

The name "Digwydd" means "to happen" in Welsh. It perfectly encapsulates my mission: to help your ideas happen, turning concepts into tangible, high-performing digital realities.
